## Executive Overview

UC Riverside's Bourns College of Engineering – Center for Environmental Research and Technology (CE-CERT) operates **one of California's most advanced vehicle testing laboratories**, designed to support the evaluation of **battery electric (BEV), plug-in hybrid (PHEV), hybrid electric (HEV), and fuel cell electric vehicles (FCEV)**.

CE-CERT's testbeds enable comprehensive performance and emissions analysis across a range of duty cycles and powertrain configurations, serving industry, government, and academic partners working toward regulatory compliance, technology validation, and real-world system optimization.

**Supported through the California Energy Commission's CalTestBed initiative**, CE-CERT provides access to **$8.8 million in testing vouchers** to clean energy innovators across nearly 30 testing facilities in California.

## Testing Capabilities Overview

CE-CERT's vehicle laboratories are equipped with **two state-of-the-art chassis dynamometers** for both **light-duty and heavy-duty electric-drive vehicles**, enabling precise, repeatable testing under simulated driving conditions with comprehensive emissions measurement capabilities.

---

## Heavy-Duty Chassis Dynamometer

### System Specifications

**Manufacturer:** Mustang Dynamometer  
**Model:** MD-AC/AC-300.48/300.48-45,000lb-HD-TANDEM  
**Design:** High-performance 48" Electric Chassis Dynamometer with **Dual Direct Connected, 300 HP AC Motors** individually attached to each roll set

### Technical Performance

| **Parameter** | **Specification** | **Application** |
|---------------|-------------------|-----------------|
| **Vehicle Weight Range** | 10,000 - 80,000 lbs | Class 4-8 heavy-duty trucks |
| **Continuous Power Absorption** | 600+ HP (45-80 mph) | Highway cruise, urban driving |
| **Intermittent Power Capacity** | 1,200 HP | High-power transient testing |
| **Base Inertia** | 45,000 lbs | Optimized load response |
| **Inertia Simulation Range** | 10,000 - 80,000 lbs | Variable weight simulation |
| **Drive Cycle Capability** | 17 different cycles | Highway, urban, certification |
| **Axle Configuration** | Tandem axle (dual roll sets) | Class 8 tractor-trailer compatibility |

### Unique Design Features

**Balanced Inertial Loading System:**
- 45,000 lbs **base inertial design** provides superior AC motor torque response
- **Trim up** for >45,000 lb inertia / **Trim down** for <45,000 lb inertia
- **Infinite inertia options** with very fast response at low speeds
- Eliminates fixed inertial plate limitations and transmission losses

**Advanced Control Capabilities:**
- **Dyno-in-the-loop** operation with open API control
- **Real-time grade and weight variation** to simulate real-world conditions
- **Real-time telemetry integration** for route optimization studies
- Mustang control system with comprehensive data acquisition

### Testing Applications

**Heavy-Duty Vehicle Types Tested:**
- Class 7-8 electric trucks (BEV)
- Hybrid natural gas buses
- Fuel cell electric commercial vehicles (FCEV)
- Plug-in hybrid heavy-duty vehicles (PHEV)
- Conventional diesel trucks (baseline comparison)
- Boom trucks with PTO/hydraulics testing capability

**Supported Drive Cycles:**
- CARB Heavy-Duty Urban Dynamometer Driving Schedule (HD-UDDS)
- EPA Federal Test Procedure (FTP-75) heavy-duty variant
- Highway cruise cycles (45-80 mph continuous)
- Custom real-world duty cycles (17 pre-programmed options)
- Transient load profiles for powertrain characterization

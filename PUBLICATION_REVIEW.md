# Publication Review Notes

This repository was organized as a public-facing professional portfolio. The current public folders contain only selected portfolio candidates.

## Public-Safe Folders

- `resume/` - current public resume copies.
- `consulting/` - small business consultation and SOP materials.
- `certifications/` - public credential proof candidates.
- `projects/ai-stack/` - local AI workbench/project overview materials.
- `projects/vetfleet/` - public VetFleet reference material.
- `projects/ce-cert/` - clean transportation and CE-CERT project overview material.
- `education/mba/` - public-safe MBA knowledge/index templates only.
- `media/` - brand assets and selected non-sensitive images.

## Held Out of Public Commit

The following categories should stay private unless manually sanitized:

- Scholarship applications, financial aid, recommendation letters, and essays with personal details.
- Transcripts, grades, student IDs, and school records.
- VA disability, DD-214, DOD ethics, military records, and benefit records.
- Tax returns, EIN letters, bank documents, insurance applications, and legal/admin filings.
- Raw OneDrive manifests, source manifests, email indexes, customer/contact lists, and internal tracking logs.
- Investor documents with unverified claims, financial projections, partner claims, or traction claims.
- Automation docs showing email credentials, app-password setup, private file paths, or internal system details.

## Local Review Area

`_PRIVATE_REVIEW_DO_NOT_COMMIT/` is ignored by Git. It can hold maps and questionable files locally for review without risking a public commit.
